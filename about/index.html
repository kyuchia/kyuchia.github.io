About


Yu-Chia Kuo is a jazz pianist and composer who loves to explore new interpretations of music. She holds a Bachelors Degree in Food Biotechnology from National Chung Hsing University, and she will study at National Taiwan Normal University for a Master of Music Technology Degree in the new semester.


She has gained vast live performance experience during her undergraduate career and specialized in composing for several media scoring projects.


Kuo’s research interests mainly focus on Music Generation and Interactive Music for multimedia. Details in her CV.
