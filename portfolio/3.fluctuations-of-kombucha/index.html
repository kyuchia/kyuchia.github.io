Fluctuations of Kombucha

Sound Design | Sonification Project | 2022

  • An interdisciplinary project combined with generative music and food biotechnology.
  • Used Max/MSP to pair experimental data with sound parameters and show the fluctuation of microorganisms in the fermentation state.
  • Converted the collected data during the Kombucha fermentation into audio and video devices by Sonification.
